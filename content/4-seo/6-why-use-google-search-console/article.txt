Here are simplified bullets of the reasons why you would want to setup Google Search Console for your site. The same points apply to Bing & Yahoo.
- Check site for errors
- See broken pages
- Check site (link: https://www.youtube.com/watch?time_continue=5&v=BNHR6IQJGZs text: indexing target:_blank)
- Submit a sitemap
- Receive messages from Google about any issues with your site.
- Help us discover problems which might hurt your search result rankings.
- Structured data testing and error reporting. What is structured data? See below.
    - Knowledge Graph (image: luke-carl-hartman-web-development-structured-data-business.png)
    - Featured Snippet (image: luke-carl-hartman-web-development-structured-data-featured-snippet.png)
    - Sitelinks (image: luke-carl-hartman-web-development-structured-data-sitelinks.png)
